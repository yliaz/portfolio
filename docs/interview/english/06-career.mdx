# 职业规划

首先要表达自己有相对清晰的职业规划，其次要说明自己对岗位的兴趣，第三要证明自己的能力和岗位要求一致。

主要内容：
- 短期目标
- 长期目标
- 职业兴趣
- 能力矩阵

## 核心句型

### 提问

<Dialog
  question="What are your career goals?"
/>

<Dialog
  question="Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"
/>

<Dialog
  question="What are your future plans?"
/>

### 短期目标

取得哪些进步，如何提升自己。

<Dialog
  answer="In the short term, I hope to gain more knowledge within the field of frontend, especially visualization."
/>

### 长期目标

核心技能，长期想做的工作岗位。

<Dialog
  answer="In the long run, I would like to use my professional skills to work as a frontend software engineer."
/>

### 职业兴趣

<Dialog
  answer="The reason is that I am really interested in autonomous driving industry."
/>

### 能力介绍

<Dialog
  answer="In addition, I take great care in the experience, architecture, and code quality of the things I build."
/>

### 与岗位挂钩

<Dialog
  answer="When I saw your job description, I knew I should apply."
/>
